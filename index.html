The Transit Payment Problem

Local transit payment systems are fragmented and stuck in the past, creating unnecessary friction for riders.

  • Multiple payment systems across different transit providers
  • Outdated technology requiring exact change or specific cards
  • No unified way to track transit spending
  • Tourists and infrequent riders face the most friction

Current transit payment systems create unnecessary friction and complexity for daily commuters and visitors alike.
